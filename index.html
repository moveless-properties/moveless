How It Works

Step 01

Property Investment

Acquiring and financing quality supported living properties through specialist lender partnerships

Step 02

Exclusive Agent Partnership

We work exclusively with a specialist agent who connects our properties with care/registered providers and provides (negotiated separately) ongoing maintenance and development services

Step 03

Heads of Terms

Heads of Terms signed between all parties establishing the framework for the lease agreement

Step 04

Lease Agreement

Establishing transparent lease terms with maintenance coordinated through our agent partner

Step 05

Final Renovations & Compliance

Final touch-ups and renovations to specifications ensuring CQC compliance and meeting GCC framework standards, including fire regulations and EPCs

Step 06

Property Delivery & Ongoing Support

Delivery of the property to Registered Providers or Care Providers with ongoing maintenance support as needed
